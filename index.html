HearBuds is a platform for users to find new friends to go to concerts with. Cartas Contra Espanol is a Spanish language learning app that uses spaced repetition to help the user learn more efficiently. A linked list is used to keep track of a users answer. This is me!

I'm a veggie farmer turned tech nerd! There are surprising similarities between farming and building programs, not the least of which is a constant vigilance for bugs!

I started a farm with my friend and then had the opportunity to spend a year working in Antarctica. After that amazing experience I came back home to Austin. I realized I wanted to have the tools to make a bigger impact on the world around me and learning to build things with computers seemed like just the tool. I love how challenging and satisfying it has been so far and look forward to the many years of learning and building ahead.

When I'm not in front of my computer I like to see live music, cook the food from my garden, hike with my dogs, ride my bicycle around Austin and play by the river. I think Austin is an amazing place!
